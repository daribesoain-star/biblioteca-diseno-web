TikTok Statistics You Need to Know
Written by Backlinko Team
Last updated on Jan. 16, 2026
Something SUPER special happened in 2016.
An app launched under two names — TikTok (global) and Douyin (China) — and began its meteoric rise.
Fast forward to 2026, and TikTok isn’t just another social media platform. It’s a juggernaut with 1.04 billion monthly active users, dominating as one of the fastest-growing networks ever.
For context: it took Facebook and Instagram nearly a decade to reach that kind of scale. TikTok? It blew past them in record time.
But the story doesn’t end there. In January 2025, TikTok faced one of its biggest challenges yet: a ban from the US government for its operations in the country. However, the app remains available in the US, and TikTok continues to lead the charge as a platform for content discovery and creativity.
Key TikTok Statistics
TikTok has 1.04 billion monthly active users worldwide.
In the US, 136 million people use TikTok.
TikTok has 82.2 million daily active users in the US.
TikTok’s US revenue reached $10 billion in 2024.
US adult users spend an average of 52 minutes per day on TikTok.
How Many People Use TikTok?
According to the latest data, TikTok had a potential ad reach of 1.94 billion users (which may not represent unique individuals) in 2025.
According to eMarketer estimates, TikTok has 1.04 billion monthly active users worldwide as of May 2024.
According to the parent company ByteDance’s announcement, TikTok hit 1 billion monthly active users in September 2021.

How Many Americans Use TikTok?
According to recent estimates, TikTok reached 82.2 million daily active users in the US in January 2025.
As of 2025, TikTok has an estimated 136 million monthly active users in the US.
According to a recent Pew Research Center study, 33% of US adults report ever using TikTok. Among American adults aged 18-29 years old, 59% report using TikTok.
Sources: Reuters , TikTok , Pew Research Center
