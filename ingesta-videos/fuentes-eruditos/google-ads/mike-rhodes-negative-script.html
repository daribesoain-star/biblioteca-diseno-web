Negative Keyword Script

Find wasted spend hiding in your search terms

Automated analysis of search terms to identify negative keyword opportunities. Reduce wasted spend without manual search term reviews.

$109 one-time • Unlimited uses • Free updates

What It Does

The Negative Keyword Script analyzes your search terms automatically and surfaces optimization opportunities you're likely missing in manual reviews.

Three types of findings:

  • •Low-performing terms eating budget
  • •Redundant negative keywords you can clean up
  • •Pattern analysis showing broader negative keyword opportunities

The Analysis

Performance Thresholds

Performance thresholds flag search terms that meet your criteria:

Low CTR (default: below 0.25% with 10+ impressions)

Identifies terms unlikely to convert.

High CPA (default: 1.5x your target)

Shows expensive conversions consuming budget on poor-fit searches.

Low ROAS (default: 0.5x your target)

Highlights unprofitable search traffic in e-commerce accounts.

All thresholds configurable based on your account performance.

Bucket Classification

Categorizes terms for easier decision-making:

  • •High conversion: Performing well, leave alone
  • •Low conversion: Converting but expensively, review carefully
  • •Zero conversion: Clicks without conversions, likely candidates

N-gram Patterns

Analyze 1, 2, and 3-word combinations across low-performing terms. Spots common words appearing in multiple poor performers—helps identify broad negatives that apply across search terms.

Redundant Negative Detection

Your negative keyword lists grow over time. Some negatives no longer receive impressions. Others overlap. Some could consolidate.

The script identifies redundant negatives:

  • •No longer receiving impressions
  • •Overlapping with other negatives in the same list
  • •Could be consolidated into fewer, broader negatives

Auto-pause option available (off by default). You control when to clean up.

AI Recommendations (Optional)

Enable AI analysis for up to 200 prioritized negative keyword suggestions.

Requires your OpenAI or Anthropic API key (bring your own). AI reviews search term patterns, performance metrics, bucket distributions, and n-gram data—then provides prioritized suggestions with reasoning.

Optional feature. Works with or without AI.
