From storyboard to animatics
This course is designed for students and young professionals in Animation, Graphic Design, Web Design, and Game Design who wish to move towards storyboarding as a profession.
Starting from their own script, or one provided by GOBELINS, students will draw a storyboard, then edit it and add sound to create a finished animatic.​
Duration
6th to 24th April 2026
Contact us
Online registration
Target audience
GOBELINS’ Online Course targets
creatives who are looking to learn or improve their storyboarding skills
and
gain inspiration
along the way.
Designed
for students and young professionals
in
Animation, Graphic Design, Web Design, and Game Design
who wish to move towards storyboarding as a profession, this course appeals also to
directors and screenwriters
wishing to understand storyboarding and its place in the animation production pipeline.
This program is
100% taught in English
, therefore applicants should have a good level of fluency to follow lectures and workshops.
Tell us more about the project you are working on at
onlinecourses@gobelins.fr
and get in touch with us for any questions you might have!

Class schedule
Classes will be held online from 4:00 P.M. to 10:00 P.M (Paris time) from 6
th
to 24
th
April 2026.
At the end of the course, participants will receive an official certificate from GOBELINS.
Tuition fees
2300 € for 3 weeks
of Lectures, Master classes, Workshop Sessions with individual tutoring.
Admission process
Applicants should complete the application form (PDF) and return it to onlinecourses@gobelins.fr with a portfolio including their projects or more advanced works to be sent via Filemail or WeTransfer.
The selection committee will examine their files for admission. The selection process remains open until places are still available.
After admissibility, the participant will receive an admission letter and will have to pay the tuition fees using our online platform in order to confirm the admission. GOBELINS will not return any submitted material.
All payments should be completed before the course starts. In case of a cancellation after the programme starts, GOBELINS reserves the right to retain full payment.
